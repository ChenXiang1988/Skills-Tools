## Quality Bar

- Do not use subjective-only criteria such as "easy to use", "beautiful", or "intuitive".
- Cover relevant loading, empty, error, permission, timeout, duplicate-action, and concurrent-state behavior.
- Link each criterion to a scenario, use case, field, metric, or explicit non-applicability reason.
