# Source Error Severity

Use this scale when original sources, knowledge-base files, or historical documents are wrong.

## P3 Minor

Small wording, naming, or formatting issue. Record and continue.

## P2 Moderate

Ambiguous or outdated information that may affect interpretation. Record the issue and add an assumption or open question.

## P1 Serious

Incorrect information that may lead to wrong PRD, prototype, diagram, or review output. Block downstream output until corrected or explicitly accepted by an owner.

## P0 Critical

Incorrect information that may cause major business, legal, financial, safety, security, or compliance harm. Stop downstream output.

## Language Rule

Be direct about the source problem, but do not attack people or groups. Criticize the material, not the person.
