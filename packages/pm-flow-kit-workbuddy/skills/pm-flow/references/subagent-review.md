# Independent Review

Review should be performed by an independent agent or reviewer that did not produce the artifact being reviewed.

## Review Targets

- Requirement analysis.
- Scenario catalog.
- Artifact plan.
- PRD.
- Prototype.
- Diagrams.
- Review artifact.

## Review Output

- Findings.
- Severity.
- Evidence.
- Recommended fix.
- Owner.
- Status.

Do not close the review stage until findings are closed or explicitly recorded as unresolved.
